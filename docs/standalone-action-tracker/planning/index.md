# Overview

This section explains how to perform planning in the Standalone Action Tracker. Planning involves managing the
categories and activities. Planning in the Standalone Action Tracker also follows the respected DHIS2 dimensions, namely
organisation units and periods.

This section explains how to setup the organisation unit and period for planning, how to create, update, and delete
categories data depending on the level of categorization selected, and the management of activities.
